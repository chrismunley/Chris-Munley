General Information

Please feel free to get in touch for any reason. If you wish to donate books to Wisdom for the Ages, please drop me an email or visit my website wisdomfortheages.com. I would be more than happy to meet with you and take whatever extra books you have off your hands.

Please only use phone contact for urgent inquiries. Otherwise, email or text is the best way to reach me.
